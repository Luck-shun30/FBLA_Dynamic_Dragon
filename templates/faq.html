Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this web app?
This web app allows users to create interactive stories by making multiple-choice decisions, with the story dynamically generated by AI.

Is the app free to use?
Yes, the app is free to use. It leverages open-source LLMs to keep costs minimal.

Do I need an account to use the app?
No, you don't need an account. You can start creating stories immediately.

What types of stories can I generate?
The app supports dynamic storytelling for various genres, including fantasy, mystery, sci-fi, and more, depending on the initial prompt and choices.

How does the app generate the story?
The app uses an open-source language model called Ollama to generate text based on user input and story context.

How is user input handled?
User inputs are processed locally or on the backend and are only used to guide the story generation. No personal data is collected.

What happens if I make contradictory choices?
The AI ensures that all choices influence the plot twist and finds a way to use all choices.

Is the story generation always original?
Yes, the AI generates unique text each time, though it may occasionally produce familiar phrases due to its training data.

Can I choose the length of the story?
The story length depends on how many choices you make. You can stop at any point or continue making choices for a longer narrative.
