About Us

Our AI Story Generator project reimagines storytelling by blending creativity and technology. This platform invites users to immerse themselves in dynamic, ever-changing narratives, making each reading experience unique. By fostering interaction and engagement, it transforms traditional reading into a participatory adventure. With a focus on accessibility and personalization, our project aims to inspire a love for stories while offering a fresh, modern twist on the classic art of storytelling.

The Team

With a strong focus on functionality and performance, he integrates complex systems seamlessly. As a co-designer, Lakshan also contributes to the platform's aesthetic and user experience, collaborating closely to create an engaging and cohesive storytelling environment.

Shankar is the creative mind behind the design and front-end development of our platform. As the designer and HTML/CSS coder, Shankar ensures that the website is visually appealing and user-friendly. Behind the Screen

Our project combines Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a robust, interactive platform. At its core, the Ollama open-source LLM generates unique stories with each interaction. We meticulously integrated these technologies to ensure a smooth, engaging user experience. Our development process was a collaborative effort, balancing backend functionality with front-end design to bring our dynamic storytelling vision to reality.
